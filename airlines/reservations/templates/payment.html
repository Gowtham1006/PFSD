FAQ's

What do I need to bring to the airport?

You will need your boarding pass and a valid photo ID (such as a passport or driver's license).

How early should I arrive at the airport?

It's recommended to arrive at least 2 hours before your domestic flight and 3 hours before your international flight.

Can I bring my pet on the flight?

Most airlines have specific policies regarding pets. Contact your airline for more information.

What happens if my flight is delayed or canceled?

If your flight is delayed or canceled, the airline will usually provide alternative arrangements. Check with airline staff for assistance.
